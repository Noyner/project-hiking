Who are we?

A community of adventurers, willing to explore and embrace nature. Love, respect and protect the environment. We are a group of people who love to hike and explore the great outdoors. We believe that hiking is not just a hobby, but a way of life. Our mission is to inspire and encourage others to get outside and experience the beauty of nature.

Overview

An unforgettable 4-day hiking experience awaits you along the Lycian Way, starting from the pine forests and turtle beach of Çıralı and finishing at the dramatic limestone walls of Göynük Canyon. Along the way we'll pass ancient ruins, hidden bays, and shaded forest trails, with turquoise sea views around every bend. Swimming breaks, unique sunsets, and local delicacies will make this experience even more special.

What Should You Bring?

  • Walking sticks (2 pieces)
  • Walking socks (with toe and heel support)
  • Hiking boots
  • Backpack (30+ liters)
  • Water and spare clothes
  • Slippers
  • Medical medications such as bandages or pain relievers/muscle relaxants
  • Buff
  • Sunglasses
  • Sunscreen
  • Towel
  • Hat
  • Fleece/jacket/raincoat
  • Snacks such as cookies, etc.
